Who we are

A physician-first AI company on a mission to eliminate diagnostic error.

Our story

How Orpheus AI was founded

Orpheus AI began in 2023 when three dermatologists and two machine-learning engineers joined forces after witnessing a tragic melanoma misdiagnosis. Late-night brainstorming sessions in a hospital cafeteria turned into a prototype that outperformed seasoned clinicians in under six months.

Today we serve health systems on three continents, but our mission hasn't changed: augment doctors, never replace them.

Our core values

Patient safety

Every line of code, every model checkpoint and UX decision is reviewed through the lens of patient outcomes.

Velocity with rigor

We iterate quickly while adhering to FDA and CE-mark quality standards.

Radical collaboration

Doctors, data scientists and designers sit side-by-side—no silos, no egos.
